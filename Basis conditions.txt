* Subspace conditions:
  - Includes the zero vector.
  - Closed under addition.
  - Closed under multiplication.

* Basis conditions:
  - Subspace.
  - Linearly independent.

* Othonormal basis conditions:
  - Subspace.
  - Linearly independant.
  - The magnitude of each vector should equal 1 (normalized).
  - The dot product of any two distinct vectors should be 0 (orthogonal).
